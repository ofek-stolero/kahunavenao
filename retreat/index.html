04 — Recovery

Ice Bath Recovery
Experience

Our guided ice bath sessions are the perfect recovery ritual after long surf sessions and active retreat days. The cold water helps reduce inflammation, accelerate muscle recovery, boost circulation, and leave your body feeling refreshed and re-energized.

Beyond the physical benefits, the ice baths also become a powerful mental experience — teaching you how to breathe through discomfort, stay present, and reconnect with your inner strength. Surrounded by nature and supported by the group's energy, it's an experience that leaves you feeling both deeply grounded and fully alive.

06 — Adventure

Iguana Island
Experience

A full day of ocean adventures, hidden beaches, and untouched island beauty. We take the boat out to a magical island surrounded by crystal-clear water, where wild iguanas roam freely and nature feels completely untouched.

It's the perfect mix of exploration, connection, sunshine, and pure freedom — the kind of experience that stays with you long after the retreat ends.

07 — Nature

Escape into the heart of Panama's natural beauty with a full-day adventure to a hidden lake and waterfall surrounded by lush jungle landscapes. 10 — Activity

Padel Wellness
Experience

As part of our wellness retreat experience, we bring the group together through fun, movement, and connection on the padel court.
