02 — Surf

Surf Experience
in Playa Venao

Whether it's your very first wave or you're already experienced in the ocean, our surf sessions are designed to meet you exactly where you are. Playa Venao offers one of the most incredible surf bays in Panama, with consistent waves for every level — from complete beginners to advanced surfers.

Our instructors guide you step by step through the foundations of surfing, helping you build confidence, independence, and a real connection with the ocean. Every surf session is professionally filmed, allowing us to provide personalized video analysis that helps each participant improve faster and understand their technique on a deeper level.

More than just a sport, surfing becomes a personal journey of growth, freedom, presence, and fun.

03 — Pilates

Pilates Studio
Experience

Move, strengthen, and reconnect with your body in one of the most unique Pilates studios you'll find in Central America. Our studio combines world-class equipment, breathtaking tropical surroundings, and an intentional wellness atmosphere designed to elevate every session into a full mind-body experience.

Equipped with premium Merrithew reformer beds — known globally for their exceptional quality and performance — our classes are suitable for every level, from complete beginners to advanced practitioners. Each session focuses on mindful movement, core strength, flexibility, posture, and creating a deeper connection with your body.

More than just a workout, our Pilates experience is designed to leave you feeling grounded, energized, aligned, and fully present.

04 — Recovery

Ice Bath Recovery
Experience

Our guided ice bath sessions are the perfect recovery ritual after long surf sessions and active retreat days. The cold water helps reduce inflammation, accelerate muscle recovery, boost circulation, and leave your body feeling refreshed and re-energized.

Beyond the physical benefits, the ice baths also become a powerful mental experience — teaching you how to breathe through discomfort, stay present, and reconnect with your inner strength. Surrounded by nature and supported by the group's energy, it's an experience that leaves you feeling both deeply grounded and fully alive.

06 — Adventure

Iguana Island
Experience

A full day of ocean adventures, hidden beaches, and untouched island beauty. We take the boat out to a magical island surrounded by crystal-clear water, where wild iguanas roam freely and nature feels completely untouched.

It's the perfect mix of exploration, connection, sunshine, and pure freedom — the kind of experience that stays with you long after the retreat ends.
